Hello there — welcome in ✨

I’m the person behind **fdkubuntu**, and this little corner of the internet is where I plan to share what I’m building, what I’m learning, and the ideas that keep following me around until I finally write them down.

A few things about me:

- I enjoy Linux a little more than is probably healthy 😄
- I like clean setups, simple tools, and websites that load fast
- I’m drawn to practical projects — the kind that solve a real problem, even if the solution looks small from the outside
- I have a soft spot for tinkering, late-night debugging, and that oddly satisfying moment when something finally clicks

This site is a fresh start.

Not a polished museum. Not a corporate brochure. Just a place to think out loud, document experiments, share projects, and leave a trail of useful things behind.

You’ll probably find posts here about:

- web projects
- Linux and dev workflows
- small technical experiments
- things I broke and then fixed the hard way
- and maybe a few opinions about what makes software feel good to use

If you’re into building, learning, or just enjoy seeing how ideas evolve from messy notes into something real, you’ll probably feel at home here.

Thanks for stopping by 💚

More soon.
